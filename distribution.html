Distribution

All of our customers receive unsurpassed delivery and distribution services. With our modern fleet of trucks and professional drivers, you know your order will arrive in prime condition. Our trucks are equipped with a (GPS) data to help our drivers avoid bad weather, accidents, and construction delays. A two-way messaging display in every cab helps ensure you’ll receive your order on time.


Be assured that we are working as a team to deliver the best customer service in the industry. Once your order has been placed, it’s important to us that you can rely upon your produce being handled properly and arriving on schedule. Fresh produce delivered on time, every time. That is our promise!

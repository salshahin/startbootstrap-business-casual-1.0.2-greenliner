Why Us?

We are committed to servicing all our clients to the fullest degree. Flexibility and consistently are our primary goals when it comes to service. 6-days a week delivery. 24-hr answering machine service. NO minimum order. NO delivery charge/fuel charge. Full Service Produce supplier covering all produce aspects of the service industry. In-house Processing Department.

We Provide:
       - Specialty and Mini Vegetables.
       - Micro Living Sprouts.
       - Organics (local and certified).
       - Seasonally Grow our own Vegetables With our on site processing department.
       - Our original concepts and our overnight processing team makes this processing plant one of a kind.
       - NO chemicals/whiteners added to product.
       - Everything prepared exactly to your specifications,
       - Overnight team ensures complete freshness delivered to you door.
       - Customizable fruit and veg blends and cut styles.
       - All products sold by pound(lb) to eliminate waste and uneven portion ratios.
Machine and artistic hand cuts available upon request.

Green Liner Produce is a customizable food service company that provide our clients with as many options and as much flexibility as possible. We intend to continue offering customization of all products, pack sizes and procedures to best suit the customers needs.

Green Liner Produce works closely with Federal and Provincial programs in terms of Food Safety Rules and Regulations. We have adopted all H.A.C.C.P procedures, and are in full compliance with all CFIA standards and regulations.
